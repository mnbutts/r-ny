bio

R&Co is a New York and London-based graphic design studio focussed on web and brand identity. R&Co collaborates with a diverse range of brands — large and small — to create tailored design and development solutions that enforce brand recognition and engagement across all platforms. R&Co takes pride in delivering meticulously crafted, visually appealing design that retains relevance over time. For more information or to discuss a project, please email info@r-ny.com.

 

Ryan Paonessa

bio

Ryan Paonessa is a New York-based cross-disciplinary designer and co-founder of R&Co. Throughout the past fifteen years, Ryan has collaborated on websites, logos and identity systems for brands of all sizes within a diverse range of industries. Specialties include Reductive visual design, Branding, UX, UI, Front-end development and more. Personal Site: rspny.com.
